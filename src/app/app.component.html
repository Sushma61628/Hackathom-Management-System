About Hackathon Management System

The Hackathon Management System is a platform designed to streamline and simplify the process of organizing and participating in hackathons. It is created to support hackathon organizers, mentors, participants, and judges.

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and creativity in the tech community. We believe that hackathons are a great way to bring together talented individuals and teams to solve real-world problems.

Key Features:

  • Team Registration
  • Mentor Registration
  • Real-time Chat
  • Jury Reviews
  • Idea Voting
  • Checkpoint Submissions
  • Hackathon Timer

Whether you are a hackathon organizer, mentor, participant, or a tech enthusiast, the Hackathon Management System is here to support and enhance your experience.

Thank you for being a part of our community!
